This agreement governs the use of the specified asset(s) and facilities provided by the lending institution. The asset(s) and facilities are to be used solely for educational purposes within the program in which the student is enrolled.

TERMS & RESPONSIBILITIES

  • I understand that assets and facilities remain the property of the lending institution and must be returned upon request or withdrawal from the program.
  • I acknowledge that assets are loaned for a specified period and agree to return them in a timely fashion.
  • I will use assets solely for non-commercial, educational purposes, which includes preparing release-related documents like resumes and release plans, while complying with DOC policies, local, state, and federal laws.
  • I will not use assets for illegal activities, unauthorized work, legal/religious/personal document creation, or activities that violate DOC policies.
  • I understand that correspondence study is permitted only when using the assets provided in the computer lab.
  • I will not create, store, or transmit prohibited content, including:
    • Escape plans and/or other information related to escape.
    • Technical/detailed information on security systems, equipment and practices used in the correctional field.
    • Any plans for activity that violates state/federal law, the Washington Administrative Code, Department policy, and/or local facility rules.
    • Any instructional and/or "how to" material for committing illegal activities.
    • Any depictions or description on the procedures for constructing or using weapons, ammunition, bombs, and/or other destructive devices, or includes life sized photograph(s)/graphic illustration(s) of these items.
    • Any narrative that provides instructions on martial arts, fighting/self-defense techniques, and/or how to disable/disarm others.
    • Any text that appears to be in code.
    • Written/graphic information on security equipment/operations or facility blueprints/diagrams.
    • Detailed maps/charts of Washington, Oregon, Idaho, and/or British Columbia.
    • Sexually explicit material as defined in WAC 137-48-020 and/or references sexually explicit behavior. May not include altered images or strategically placed graphics.
    • Gang symbols or symbols of other unauthorized groups that may reasonably be thought to precipitate violence.
    • Games, videos, and messages that are not part of the education program.
  • I will not encrypt or password-protect any files on the assets provided by the institution, to maintain transparency and avoid the appearance of concealing unauthorized files or usage.
  • I will use only institution-approved software and programs on electronic devices and will not access prohibited websites or online services.
  • I will not cheat, misrepresent my own work, or allow others to do my work.
  • I will not lend, share, or transfer any assets, accounts, passwords, or files to others without explicit authorization from the institution.
  • I will not use any assets, accounts, or files assigned to others without explicit authorization from the institution.
  • I will not deface, destroy, misuse, or alter any assets. This includes unauthorized repairs, modifications, or disabling of security features.
  • I will handle all assets with care, ensuring they are not subjected to damage from extreme conditions, rough handling, dropping, exposure to liquids, or being left outdoors or unattended. Additionally, I will avoid using any assets near food or drink to prevent potential damage.
  • I am responsible for any damage, loss, or costs associated with the repair or replacement of the assets due to my intentional or negligent handing.
  • I will treat books and printed materials with care and use consumables like paper responsibly for educational purposes only.
  • Before returning the asset(s), I will not remove any data, personal or otherwise, to ensure full transparency and enable appropriate inspection by authorized individuals.
  • I will keep all accounts and passwords secure and confidential.
  • I will ensure that assets are always in my possession or in a secure location.
  • While using any provided assets, I will ensure my personal information remains confidential.
  • I will bring any assigned asset(s) to school, ensuring they are available for educational purposes as directed by the instructor.
  • I will promptly report any damage, malfunction, or loss of assets, as well as any concerns related to data stored on assets.
  • I will inform the institution in case of withdrawal or transfer from the program to facilitate the timely return or transfer of assets as necessary.
  • I will comply with all inspection procedures for the assets as set by the institution.
  • I understand that failure to adhere to these terms in full may result in consequences, including loss of asset usage privileges, potential impact on educational program participation, and disciplinary action to include serious infraction as defined in WAC 137-25-030.

Grays Harbor College reserves the right to interpret and enforce these rules in a manner that aligns with the policies of the Department of Corrections, ensuring compliance and maintaining the integrity of our educational programs.

ACKNOWLEDGMENT & AGREEMENT

The student hereby acknowledges their understanding of, and agreement to, the terms and conditions outlined in this document. By signing, the student commits to adhering to these responsibilities and acknowledges that failure to comply with any of these terms may result in immediate consequences. These consequences include, but are not limited to, the voiding of this agreement, potential disciplinary infractions as per Department of Corrections (DOC) policies, revocation of access to facilities and assets, and the possibility of being dropped from the education program. Such actions will be taken to uphold the integrity and security of the educational environment within the correctional facility.
